In Korean, when you meet people, say "안녕하세요" at any time. Koreans don't use "Good morning", "Good evening", "Good afternoon".




Some people use "좋은 아침"(Good morning), but it's not common.

안녕하세요 [annyeonghaseyo]

When you part with someone, there are 2 types of greeting.
If you leave, you can say "안녕히 계세요".
If you stay and guests leave, you can say "안녕히 가세요".

안녕히 가세요[annyeonghi gaseyo]
Good bye, When you stay

안녕히 계세요[annyeonghi gyeseyo]
Good-bye, When you leave

When listener and speaker both leave, say "안녕히 가세요"

안녕히 가세요[annyeonghi gaseyo]
Good bye,
