 ~랑[lang]  with, together

오빠랑[oppalang]  with brother
오빠[opp]  elder brother of girl

친구랑[chingulang]  with friends
친구[chingu]  friend




서상사님, 신지영이란 분한테 소포 왔는데,
seosangsanim, sinjiyeong-ilan bunhante sopo wassneunde
Sergeant Seo. You have a package from someone named Shin Jae Young.
~ 님 honorific suffix
-ex)선생님[seonsaengnim] teachter
-ex)기사님[gisanim] driver
소포[sopo] package


어디계신가요? 오버
eodigyesingayo? obeo
Where are you? Over.
Honorific speech


지금 어디십니까?
jigeum eodisibnikka?
Where are you right now?
지금[jigeum] now
formal, Honorific speech


막사 식당 쪽이요
magsa sigdang jjog-iyo
At the kitchen


기다릴까요? 오버
gidalilkkayo? obeo
Shall I wait, over?
기다리다[gidalida] v.wait


어 깜짝아
eo kkamjjag-a
Oh! You surprised me.


이거 제가 확인 좀 해야겠습니다
igeo jega hwag-in jom haeyagessseubnida
I have to check this.
이거[igeo] this
제가[jega] humble word of 'I', the subjective
확인[hwag-in] n.check


오빠, 힘내세요 하트
oppa, himnaeseyo hateu
Oppa, keep strong. Heart?


보고 싶어요, 오빠, 하트!
bogo sip-eoyo, oppa, hateu!
I miss you, oppa. Heart?!


서상사님, 여동생 있어?
seosangsanim, yeodongsaeng iss-eo?
Does Sergeant Seo have a younger sister?
여동생[yeodongsaeng] younger sister
~있어? Do S have(has)~?


서상사 외동이거든요!
seosangsa oedong-igeodeun-yo!
Sergeant Seo is an only child!


그게 내 잘못은 아닐텐데?
geuge naejalmos-eun aniltende?
That will not be my fault.
그게[geuge] that
내(my) 잘못(fault) [nae jalmo]


그래도 돼?
geulaedo dwae?
Can you do that?


허, 어쭈!
heo, eojju!
Huh, How dare he!.


뭐야? 유시진이 왜 여기있어?
mwoya? yusijin-i wae yeogiiss-eo?
What is it? Why is Yoo Si Jin here?
왜[wae] why
여기(here) 있어?(~is) [yeogi iss-eo]


시진 오빠랑 만났던 날
sijin oppalang mannassdeonnal
The day I met Si Jin oppa.
오빠랑[oppalang] with oppa(direct translation)
날[nal] day


우리의 추억들을 보내요
uliui chueokdeul-eul bonaeyo
I send you our memories
우리의(our) 추억들(memories) [uliui chueokdeul]
~보내요[bonaeyo] polite form of 'send'


이 남자들이!
i namjadeul-i!
These guys..!
이(this) 남자들(guys, men) [i namjadeul]


윤중위, 총 가져와
yunjung-wi, chong gajyeowa
Lieutenant Yoon, bring your gun.
총[chong] gun
