One Point Lesson!
나 [na]   I
내가[naega]  the subjective case of 나
- the subject of acting
- emphasize 'I'

제가[jega]  humble term of 내가

for example.

a:산타는 누가 할래? [santaneun nuga hallae]
   (Who wants to be Santa?)
b:내가 할래.[naega hallae]
   (I will be Santa)


a: 누가 내 아이스크림 먹었어?[nuga nae aiseukeulim meog-eoss-eo?]
   (Who ate my icecream?)
b: 내가 먹었어 [naega meog-eoss-eo ]
   (I ate..) 

In this scene, Do Min-ik uses informal words and Jung Gal-hee uses polite word. Let's start! 

Jung Gal-hee>
아, 네, 제가 갈께요
a, ne, jega galkkeyo
Okay, I'll be right there.
-가다[gada] (v)go


Jung Gal-hee>
어, 죄송해요
eo, joesonghaeyo
Oh, I'm sorry.
(Gal-hee spills water)
Domin-ik>
어 아냐, 아냐, 괜찮어
eo anya, anya, gwaenchanh-a 
No, No, it's okay. I'm fine.

Jung Gal-hee>
다 젖었네. 
da jeos-eossne
It's all wet. 
-다[da] all
옷 좀 벗어보세요
os jom beos-eoboseyo
Take off your clothes.
-옷[os] clothes


Domin-ik>
벗어?
beos-eo?
Take them off? 
-벗다[beosda] (v)take off
(벗어보세요. 벗어요. 벗어, 벗어, 벗어)
(beos-eoboseyo. beos-eoyo. beos-eo, beos-eo, beos-eo)
(Take off your pants.Take them off. Take them off. Take off, Take them off)
Jung Gal-hee>
네 제가 얼른 말려 드릴테니까
ne jega eolleun mallyeo deuliltenikka
Yes. I'll dry them for you quickly.
-얼른[eolleun] quickly
-말리다[mallida] (v)dry


Domin-ik>
분명.. 당신이 벗으라 그랬다
bunmyeong dangsin-i beos-eula geulaessda
obviously, You're the one...who told me to take them off. You're the one...
-분명[bunmyeong] definitely
-당신[dangsin] you


Jung Gal-hee>
네?
ne?
What?
Domin-ik>
나는..잘 참고 있었는데
naneun..jal chamgo iss-eossneunde
I was..trying hard to restrai
-참다[chamda] (v) hold back.


Domin-ik>
당신이 벗으라 그랬다구
dangsin-i beos-eula geulaessdagu
But you told me to take them off
Jung Gal-hee>
아 본부장님 그게 그런 뜻이 아니라
a bonbujangnim  geuge geuleontteus-i anila
Director Do, that's not what I meant.
뜻[tteus] (n)meaning
그런 뜻이 아니라 that's what I meant
Domin-ik>
난 정말.. 나는 진짜 ...잘
nan jeongmal.. naneun jinjja ...jal
I was really...I was...truly...
-정말[jeongmal] really
-진짜[jinjja] really, truly
