One point Lession
In many Korean conversation, You can see the use of '우리'[uri] word. It means 'we' or 'our'. and when it is used with noun, it means 'our', like 우리 딸'(our daughter), '우리 엄마'(our mom). Of cause, it is interpreted into "my" for sentence meaning.
It's a characteristic of Korean language. In Korean language, people use 'our' word instead of 'my' word in common. Let's see some example.

우리(our) 엄마(mom) [uli eomma] 
means 'my mom'.
Even when a child is a only child in his(her) family, the kid uses '우리 엄마'

우리(our) 학교(school) [uli haggyo] 
means 'my school'

우리(our) 딸(daughter) [uli ttal] 
means 'my daughter'

우리(our) 집(house)[uli jib]  means 'my house'
Even when a person lives alone, he(she) says '우리 집'
