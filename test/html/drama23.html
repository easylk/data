One Point Lesson!
What are you doing?  뭐해요?

내일 뭐해요? [naeil mwohaeyo]
What are you doing tomorrow?
 -내일[naeil]  tomorrow

오늘 뭐해요?  [oneul mwohaeyo?]
What are you doing today?
 -오늘[oneul]  today

토요일에 뭐해요?  [toyoil-e mwohaeyo]
What are you doing this Saturday?
-토요일[toyoil] saturday



저 전구
jeo jeongu
That light bulb..
저(That) 전구(light bulb)


왜 안갈았습니까?
wae angal-assseubnikka?
Why didn't you change it?
왜[wae] why
갈다[galda] v.change


아?
a?
A....


키 커지면 갈려고, 손이 안닿아서
ki keojimyeon gallyeogo, son-i andah-aseo
I'm going to change it when I become tall, because I can't reach it
키[ki] n.stature
손[son] n.hand


내가 갈아 줄까요?
naega gal-a julkkayo?
Shall I change it for you?
내가 the subject form of 'I'


그거 한번만 더 해주면 안되요?
geugeo hanbeonman deo haejumyeon andoeyo?
Can you do that one more time?
그거[geugeo] that
한번만(just one time) 더(more)
한번[hanbeon] one time


그거?
geugeo?
That?


셔츠 딱! 시계 딱! 그거
syeocheu ttag! sigye ttag! geugeo
You unbuttoned your shirt like this, ttag! and unclasped your watch, ttag!
셔츠[syeocheu] shirt
시계[sigye] watch


아 뭐 이런 걸로
a mwo ileon geollo
It's nothing.


(laughing)


멋있고 섹시한데 웃겨
meos-issgo segsihande usgyeo
It's cool and sexy, but funny
멋있다[meos-issda] be cool
섹시하다[segsihada] sexy


웃기고 귀여운데 또 멋있어
usgigo gwiyeounde tto meos-iss-eo
It's funny and cute, but it's also cool.
웃기다[usgida] be funny
귀엽다[gwiyeobda] be cute


내일 뭐해요?
naeil mwohaeyo?
What are you doing tomorrow?
내일(tomorrow) 뭐해요?(What are you doing?)
일해요
ilhaeyo
work
일하다[ilhada] v.work


일은 나도 하는데
il-eun nado haneunde
I'll work, too.
일[il] n.working


왜요?
waeyo?
Why?


일하기 싫어서요
ilhagi silh-eoseoyo
Because I don't want to work.


같이 일 안할수 있나 하고
gat-i il anhalsu issna hago
I wondered if we could skip work together.
같이[gat-i] together


대박, 일하기 싫단 말 처음 듣네?
daebag, ilhagi silhdan mal cheoeum deudne?
Wow, This is the first time I heard that you don't want to work.


왠일이야?
waen-il-iya
What is happening?


서도재씨, 나 엄청 좋아하나봐
seodojaessi, na eomcheong joh-ahanabwa
You must really like me.
엄청[eomcheong] very much


네
ne
Yes.


그런거 같습니다
geuleongeo gatseubnida
I think so.
